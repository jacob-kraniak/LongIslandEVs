# Long Island EV Community Discord

An independent, volunteer-run Discord server for electric vehicle (EV) enthusiasts, owners, and future adopters on Long Island (Nassau & Suffolk counties).

This server provides a friendly, local hub to:
- Share charging spot recommendations and experiences
- Discuss EV ownership tips, maintenance, and model comparisons
- Coordinate ride-and-drives, meetups, and group events
- Stay updated on Long Island–specific charging infrastructure, incentives, and policy news
- Connect with other EV drivers in the Metro NY region

## Goals

Build an active, welcoming space that helps accelerate EV adoption across Long Island by making it easier to connect, share knowledge, and reduce barriers for new and existing EV drivers.

## Contributing

This is a volunteer-run community. Ways to help:
- Welcome new members
- Share accurate, local EV info
- Suggest channels or bots that improve the experience
- Moderate when needed (DM an admin if interested)

All are welcome — current EV owners, lease drivers, people considering a switch, or just EV-curious residents.

Questions or suggestions? Join the server and post in #general or #suggestions.
